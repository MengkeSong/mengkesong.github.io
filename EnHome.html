About me

I am currently pursuing my PhD in the College of Computer Science and Technology, China University of Petroleum (East China), under the supervision of Professor C. Chen (ChengLizhao Chen). Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in School of Information Science and Technology, Beijing Language and Culture University in June 2018, and master's degree from School of Computer Science and Technology, Qingdao University in June 2023.

Research Interests: Computer Vision, Virtual Reality, 360 Panoramic Video Navigation, Saliency Detection, Graph Neural Network.
