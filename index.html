About me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D in the College of Computer Science and Technology, China University of Petroleum (East China), under the supervision of Professor Chenglizhao Chen. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from the College of Information Science and Technology, Beijing Language and Culture University in June 2018, and my master's degree from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Qingdao University in June 2023.

Research Interests: Affective Computing (Emotion/Depression/Pain Level/Personality Recognition), Virtual Reality, (RGB/RGB-D/RGB-T/Audio-Video) Salient Object Detection/Ranking, 360° Panoramic Video Navigation, Image Quality/Aesthetics Assessment, Video/Industrial Anomaly Detection, Monocular Depth Estimation, Graph Neural Network.
